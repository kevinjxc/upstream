Our Programs

STREAM Labs
  • STREAM Labs are free in-person workshops hosted multiple times each year, allowing students to explore STREAM through a hands-on approach.

    • Next Session: The Robotics Lab (April 8)
    • 12:00 PM to 3:00 PM at the Beacon Hill Library

  • Students in this program utilize engineering, robotics, or coding to bring a creation to life and engage in public speaking through a debate.

LiveSTREAMs
  • LiveSTREAMs are free online workshops hosted at the end of each month, allowing students to explore STREAM from a virtual setting.

    • Next Session: Artificial Intelligence (February 24)
    • 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM at an Online Meeting Link

  • Students in this program utilize various STREAM principles to bring a creation to life while learning about societal connections behind different STREAM topics.

Online Tutoring
  • Online Tutoring offers free tutoring sessions through a virtual setting, allowing students to supplement their academic performance.

    • Weekdays: After 4:00 PM for Reading or Mathematics
    • Weekends: After 9:00 AM for Writing, Reading, or Mathematics

  • Students in this program engage in 1:1 sessions with a qualified Seattle high school student, providing an excellent source of instructional guidance.
