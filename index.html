Welcome to UpSTREAM!

UpSTREAM teaches STREAM (Science, Technology, Robotics, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics) to Seattle students in grades 3-5. Our work is bridging resource gaps caused by disproportionate access to STREAM opportunities through accessible and culturally-relevant STREAM education. Founded by students driven by their ambition to contribute to their community, UpSTREAM offers three free STREAM programs: STREAM Labs, LiveSTREAMs, and Online Tutoring.

STREAM Workshops

UpSTREAM provides free STREAM workshops for students in grades 3-5, including our seasonal in-person STREAM Labs and monthly online LiveSTREAMs.
  • Next LiveSTREAM: Hour of Code, June 28 from 1 PM to 2 PM
  • Next STREAM Lab: The Robotics Lab, June 30 from 12 PM to 3 PM
Students in these workshops engage in hands-on activities involving STREAM topics ranging from engineering to robotics in societal and cultural contexts.

Online Tutoring

UpSTREAM provides free online tutoring for students in grades 3-5. Online Tutoring allows students to supplement their academic performance.
  • Weekdays: After 4 PM for STREAM or Humanities
  • Weekends: After 9 AM for STREAM or Humanities
Students in this program engage in 1:1 sessions with a knowledgeable Seattle high school student, providing an excellent source of mentorship.
